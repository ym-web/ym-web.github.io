We are going to exploring the manufacturing of the United States on this site.

Manufacturing is one of the most essential parts of our economy. Manufacturing does not only provide jobs for millions of people but also plays a vital role in affecting the global competitiveness of a country. The United States had become the largest manufacturing power in the world since the end of the Great War. For more than a half century, the United States had been a super power that provided other countries products ranging from commercial airplane to microelectronic chips.

The golden age of manufacturing in the States is at its end, however. The economical growth of the States has increasingly relied on service related sections and the output of the manufacturing related sections has been stagnating over years.
